Welcome to Brasswild
Build Your Civilization

Build a unique civilization, develop and explore new horizons. Team up in pairs and perfect your cities over two peaceful months.

In our peaceful world, you can grow your civilization without threats from other players. Progress at your own pace, engage in construction, trade, and diplomacy. Create a unique city that will be your pride.
Trade with Other Players

Engage in trade with other players, exchange resources and rare items. Find partners and strike deals to advance your civilization.

Trading is key to prosperity. Open markets, find rare resources, and build economic connections. Collaborate with other players to strengthen your civilization and reach new heights. Share experiences and achieve mutual benefit.
Run Events

Organize exciting events: races, sports competitions, shows, and gladiator battles. Have fun and compete in a friendly atmosphere.

Entertainment is an essential part of life on the server. Compete in creative events, showcase your skills, and win prizes. Inspire other players and enjoy unforgettable moments with friends. Spend your time having fun and making memories!
Using Mods

Enhance your gameplay with major mods like Create, Steam 'n' Rails, Crafts & Additions, and Farmer's Delight. Enjoy optimized performance with Fabric and mods like Sodium, Indium, and Iris.

Our server runs on the Fabric mod loader, providing a seamless and optimized experience. Dive into creative builds with Create, explore new mechanics with Steam 'n' Rails, and enjoy enhanced farming with Farmer's Delight. Performance mods like Sodium and Iris ensure smooth gameplay, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in your civilization-building journey.
